# Scene 01 — Action Combat

Steel claws shrieked against warped air as Aurora twisted her will through the fabric of gravity. The corrupted *nue*, its body a grotesque weave of serpent-tail and beastial limbs, recoiled mid-lunge, its own momentum turned against it. Space bent around her like a bowstring, and with a sharp exhale, she wrenched the yokai sideways—crashing it into a shattered torii gate. "Now! Its essence is frayed!" she called, voice taut with effort, palms still splayed as invisible tides coiled at her command.

From the smoke, Kaito surged forward, twin *chijiragis* ablaze with foxfire. "On you, Aurora!" He vaulted over splintered wood, blades carving twin arcs toward the dazed monster. But the *nue* shrieked—a sound like tearing silk—and thrashed, unleashing a wave of miasma. Vines of blackened *kami* lashed outward. 

Mira dropped from the temple eaves like a shadow given form. Her *wakizashi* flashed, severing the tendrils before they could ensnare Kaito. "Too slow, furball!" she spat, landing in a crouch. The air thrummed with unstable power—Aurora’s graviturgy humming like distant thunder, Kaito’s fire flaring brighter, Mira’s blade whispering with ancestral chants. The *nue* writhed, caught between forces of balance and decay.  

"Break its core!" Aurora commanded, eyes aglow with indigo force. She braced—reality itself groaned—and began to compress the space around the beast’s twisted heart. The final strike loomed, swift and merciless.
