# Scene 06 — Comedic Banter

As the visiting ambassador from the Land of the Setting Sun rose to depart, Koibo, in a moment of unbridled enthusiasm, attempted to bow deeply, but his usually reliable yokai magic had other plans. To the horror of the assembled court, Koibo's body began to contort and shift, his limbs merging into a delicate ceramic form as he transformed into a teapot. The ambassador's eyes widened in confusion, while the usually stoic Emperor struggled to stifle a grin.

The teapot, still bearing Koibo's flustered expression, sat perched upon the polished wooden floor, its lid opening and closing in a futile attempt to speak. The court erupted into a mixture of gasps and stifled giggles, as the ambassador, unsure how to react, bowed low to the teapot, saying, "I... I thank you for the, ah, unique refreshment, honorable... teapot?" The Emperor, now openly chuckling, patted the ambassador's shoulder, saying, "Fear not, dear guest, Koibo's... creative transformations are a, shall we say, 'traditional' aspect of our court."

As the teapot Koibo continued to splutter and whistle, the Emperor waved a hand, and a nearby servant rushed to pour a stream of soothing incense into the air, calming the magical energies and allowing Koibo to revert to his true form. Rubbing his temples, Koibo bowed deeply, muttering, "I... apologize for the, ah, 'steeped' impression, your excellencies." The ambassador, now laughing openly, clapped Koibo on the back, saying, "I daresay, this has been the most... enlightening audience of my diplomatic career."
